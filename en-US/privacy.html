Heart Surround Privacy Policy

Updated: 2026-05-04

Heart Surround is a locally running interactive story app. The current version does not require account registration and does not include in-app purchases, ads, third-party analytics SDKs, or user tracking.

Story progress, affection values, saves, settings, and unlock states are stored only on your device to provide continuing play, replay, and personalized reading features.

The current version does not actively collect, upload, or share personal information, and does not access location, contacts, photos, camera, microphone, health data, or other sensitive permissions.

If a later version adds online features, accounts, cloud sync, or third-party services, this policy and the App Store privacy information will be updated before release.

For help, please visit the support page.
