So Good: Beauties Around Me Support

Thank you for using So Good: Beauties Around Me. If you experience story progress, save, display, or device compatibility issues, please prepare your device model, iOS version, app version, and screenshots, then contact the developer through the App Store support channel.

Common steps:

1. Confirm that your system is updated to a supported version.
2. Close and reopen the app.
3. Adjust subtitles, auto-play, or font size on the Settings page.
4. To replay the story from the beginning, reset current progress on the Settings page.
